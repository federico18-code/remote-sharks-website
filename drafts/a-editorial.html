02 · The Process

From kickoff to shortlist in 7 to 10 days.

No black box, no ghosting, no fifty-page reports. A tight funnel where every candidate you meet has already earned their place.

  1. Step 01

    Role deep-dive

    We start with a working session on the role: outcomes, must-haves, culture, and the real reason the seat exists. The brief we write together drives everything downstream.

  2. Step 02

    Targeted sourcing

    Active outreach on LinkedIn Recruiter, Upwork, and local LATAM platforms, amplified with paid ads on all three. We fish where the strong swimmers are, not just where it is convenient.

  3. Step 03

    Video screening

    Every promising candidate records a structured video screen. Communication, English, and presence are assessed before anyone spends a minute of your time.

  4. Step 04

    Founder interview

    I interview the top candidates personally, live. Depth of skill, motivation, and fit against your brief. Nothing gets delegated at this stage.

  5. Step 05

    Shortlist of three

    You receive three vetted finalists with clear notes on strengths, trade-offs, and my honest recommendation. Quality over volume, always.

  6. Step 06

    You meet the finalists

    We coordinate the interviews, you make the call. I stay close through offer and onboarding so nothing slips between shortlist and start date.
