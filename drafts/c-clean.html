How it works

Kickoff to shortlist in four steps.

No black box. You know exactly where your search stands at every stage.

  1. STEP 01

    Role deep-dive

    We start with a working session on the role: outcomes, must-haves, deal-breakers, budget, and what "great" looks like on your team.

  2. STEP 02

    Targeted sourcing

    We hunt across LinkedIn Recruiter, Upwork, and local LATAM platforms, and run paid ads on all of them to widen the pool beyond who is actively looking.

  3. STEP 03

    Double vetting

    Every promising candidate passes a structured video screening, then a live interview with the founder. Only proven skills and real English make it through.

  4. STEP 04

    Your shortlist of 3

    Within 7 to 10 days of kickoff you meet three finalists, each with notes on strengths, rates, and availability. You interview, you choose, you hire.

FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

Short, honest answers. Anything else, bring it to the call.

How fast will I see candidates?

You will have a shortlist of 3 vetted finalists within 7 to 10 days of kickoff. The clock starts after the role deep-dive, so the better we define the role together, the faster the search moves.

Why hire from Latin America?

Three reasons: your time zone, not a 12-hour offset; strong English across senior professionals; and rates that let you hire genuinely experienced people without stretching the budget. You get real-time collaboration instead of overnight handoffs.

How does pricing work?

Simple 50/50 split. Half of the fee starts the search, and the other half is due only when you hire someone from the shortlist. No retainers and no monthly subscriptions. We quote the fee up front on your kickoff call based on the role.

What if none of the finalists is a fit?

Then we go back into the pool and keep searching. The second half of the fee is only ever due when you actually hire, so an unfilled shortlist costs you nothing extra and costs us time. That keeps our vetting honest.

Ready to meet your next hire?

Bring the role. In 20 minutes we will tell you if we can fill it, how long it will take, and exactly what it costs. If we are not the right fit, we will say so.
